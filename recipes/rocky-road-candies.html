Rocky Road Candies

Description

A tasty chocolate treat. I couldn't be easier to make this dessert. Just melt chocolate chips and stir in peanuts and marshmallows. Enjoy a classic family favorite!

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) package semisweet chocolate chips
  • ⅛ cup butter
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 ½ cups dry-roasted peanuts
  • 1 (16 ounce) package miniature marchmallows

Directions

  1. Line a 9 x 13 inch pan with wax paper.
  2. In a microwave-safe bowl, microwave chocolate and butter until melted. Stir occasionally until chocolate is smooth.
  3. Stir in condensed millk. Combine peanuts and marshmallows; stir into chocolate mixture.
  4. Pour into prepared pan and chill until firm. Cut into squares.
