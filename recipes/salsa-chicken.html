Salsa Chicken

Description

A tasty Mexican-themed meal! Frozen chicken breasts are combined with taco seasoning and salsa for an easy weeknight meal. You can use the meat for tacos, burritos, or as a topping for baked potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound frozen skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • 1 ounce packet taco seasoning mix
  • ½ cup salsa
  • ½ cup low-sodium chicken broth

Steps

  1. Place chicken breasts in an Instant Pot. Sprinkle all sides with taco seasoning. Pour salsa and chicken broth on top.
  2. Close and lock lid. Select Poultry setting; set timer for 15 minutes. Allow 10 to 15 minutes for pressure to build.
  3. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 20 minutes. Unlock and remove the lid.
  4. Shred chicken and serve as desired.
